The leader drops to the ground, bleeding and motionless. Reginold hacks down another with his sword.
The remaining three run away, whooping and yipping.

Their cries are joined by others from the dark forest all around you. Many calls from far too many 
goblins to fight toe-to-toe.

"It's a hunting party Probably tracking wild game. We humans are a delicacy, much better than 
mutton." Reginold says.

You regard the two dead goblins on the ground. Dead goblins are worth ten gold pieces per head in the
city. Luckily, you don't have to carry their heads around. The city authorities will accept their left ears.

What will you do?
