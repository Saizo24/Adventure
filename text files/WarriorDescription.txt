The Warrior Class

The warrior is the strongest class and a master of all melee combat. With his heavy armor, he can take on
every hit. He relies on his strength to overwhelm his foes.

Starting Attributes:
Strength: 8
Dexterity: 3
Intelligenz: 0

Health Points: 30
Mana Points: 0

Wearable:
Light, medium, heavy armor and shields
