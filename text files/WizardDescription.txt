The Warrior Class

The Wizard has devoted his life to his studies on arcane magic. His knowledge on spells
are diverse and unsurpassed. He uses his magical energy called mana to fuel his spells.

A Wizard generates a Shield each combat to protect himself at the cost of a bit of Mana.

Starting Attributes:
Strength: 0
Dexterity: 3
Intelligenz: 10

Shield Points: 30
Health Points: 20
Mana Points: 30

Wearable:
Light Armor
