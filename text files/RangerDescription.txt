The Ranger Class

Although not so strong in close combat, the ranger is the undisputed master of the bow. Though
they need lighter armor, so it doesn't interfere with the movement of the ranger. 

The ranger can dish out two attacks per Round.

Starting Attributes:
Strength: 3
Dexterity: 10
Intelligenz: 0

Health Points: 30
Mana Points: 0

Wearable:
Light, medium armor and shields
